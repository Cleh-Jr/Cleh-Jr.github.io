Descrição

Welcome to Arcade Paradise, the 90’s retro arcade adventure game. With the keys to the family laundromat, Ashley decides to transform the business and give the sleepy town of Grindstone something to get excited about! Take the laundromat business made up of dull chores from washing clothes to throwing out the trash, and turn it into a booming arcade with the coolest games in town raking in all the money! Stay one step ahead of your father, manage and invest profits, and build your very own Arcade Paradise!

In addition to building your own arcade center, there are over 35 arcade games in Arcade Paradise, each fully realized with its own gameplay, stories, missions and high scores to set! Inspired by 3 decades of gaming, these games offer everything from early vector games right up to the 32-bit era. Insert a second coin and play against a friend in a number of co-operative & competitive arcade games up to 4 players locally. Prove yourself across each game to achieve high scores, set your initials, and make your favorite arcade game more popular -- you can even upload your scores to the online leaderboards! From the individual games to choosing what song to put on the jukebox inspired by some of the great records of the early 90s, the Arcade Paradise soundtrack captures the heart of a bygone era.
