Descrição

South of the Circle is a narrative adventure, telling the story of Peter, a Cambridge academic who finds himself fighting for survival in Antarctica during the 1960’s Cold War. Peter flashes back to his life at the university and his relationship with a fellow lecturer, Clara. As Peter battles against the harsh conditions and his attempts to escape the ice become more desperate, the lines between his past and present begin to blur. South of the Circle is a unique experience about memory, survival, and the consequences of not dealing with the past.
