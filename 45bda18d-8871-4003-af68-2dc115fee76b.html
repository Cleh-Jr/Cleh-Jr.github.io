Descrição

Horizon Chase Turbo is the new version of the acclaimed retro racing game Horizon Chase: World Tour, a modern take on '90s old-school arcade racing games. The game features unique art direction, with a bright and colorful palette that mixes low-poly environments and highly detailed 2D backgrounds. Play with your friends in a split-screen local multiplayer campaign, race against the best times in the online Ghost Mode in 110 tracks around the world!

Composed by Barry Leitch (Top Gear, Lotus and Rush series), the soundtrack is uniquely techno-inspired and energetic. What really sets Horizon Chase apart from other racing games is the blend of its arcade style and the strong sense of actual exploration and discovery that the player feels through its many and quite diverse circuits, many of them dominated by natural landscapes.
