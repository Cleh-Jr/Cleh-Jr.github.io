Descrição

Open your own Card shop and buy, trade, & sell cards (or keep them for your own collection) in the card shop simulator game Kardboard Kings. Earn reputation with customers, beat your shop goals, unlock new decorations, and host exciting events to bring everyone to the best card shop in town! New management means new branding -- create your dream shop with a catalogue of walls, flooring, furniture, and knickknacks. Collect over 100 unique cards -- rom ripping open booster packs, to buying singles, you’ll uncover dozens of vibey cards featuring illustrations from stacks of talented artists. (Plus their rare, shiny variants!) Reprints, rumors, rule changes, kids' meal promos, thieves, and church condemnations (the usual suspects) will all affect the price of your treasured cards. Think fast! Befriend regulars by tracking down their requests, help build their decks, and become a part of a sleepy seaside town! Or just rip them off and feel their wrath! And just when you think you've got a master plan for business, you will find that all is not what it seems on the seaside! The appearance of a mysterious masked thief leaves locals wondering who to trust with their precious cards! Who is this shadowy character? And just what are the Legendary Cards?
