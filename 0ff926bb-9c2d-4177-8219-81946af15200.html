Descrição

In Ghostbusters: Spirits Unleashed, be the buster or be the ghost in 4v1 asymmetrical multiplayer hunts! Play as a Ghostbuster, strap on a Proton Pack and join a team of four brave souls pursuing ghastly ghosts terrorizing public locations. Work together to find the Ghost, monitoring the trusty PKE Meter to find the spectral energies, then blast them with streams from the Particle Thrower to tether the slimy foe in place, and shut the trap at the perfect moment! Keep citizens calm and corral the Ghost before the environment gets too haunted. Or play as a Ghost, scaring unsuspecting citizens at various locations. You ain’t afraid of no Ghostbuster, thanks to the ability to fly and teleport between rifts. Possess objects lying around each room to sneak by roaming Ghostbusters and frighten passersby. If that doesn’t work, sliming and summoning ghoulish minions will. Haunt each map to completion as one of multiple Ghosts with different abilities.

Prepare for each match in the iconic Firehouse by customizing your Ghostbuster or Ghost, upgrading equipment and abilities, and getting some target practice in using the Particle Thrower as well as its different modifications. Talk with Winston Zeddemore, voiced by Ernie Hudson, and get missions before going out into the field. Meet some new and old friends like Dan Aykroyd’s Ray Stantz, ready to hand down some busting wisdom at Ray’s Occult Books. Thanks to cross-platform multiplayer, anyone on any supported platform can come together to cross the streams or spew slime. AI companions can help fill games. You can even enable solo Ghostbusters or a solo Ghost to play offline.
