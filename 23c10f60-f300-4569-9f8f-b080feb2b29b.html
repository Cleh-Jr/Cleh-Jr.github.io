Descrição

Now you can take your D&D action to new heights with Advanced Dungeons & Dragons: Eye of the Beholder. The town of Waterdeep has been shrouded in darkness by an evil force that is thought to be ruling the sewers underneath the city streets. You must lead a party of four noble souls who are willing to risk their lives in order to free Waterdeep from the clutches of this unknown evil. You can build your party from six different species Humans, Elves, Half-Elves, Gnomes, Dwarves, and Halflings each with their own unique abilities and characteristics. Finding the right mix of party members is the key to forming an unbeatable force that can vanquish the foes you'll face. Through the point-and-click interface, you'll explore the dark and dank labyrinth of the sewers, fighting skeletons, kolbolds and who knows what else along the way. The town of Waterdeep is counting on your courage, strength, and intelligence to save it from the evil that lurks below.
