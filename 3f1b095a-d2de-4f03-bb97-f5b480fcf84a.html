Descrição

Street Fighter 6 represents the next evolution of the Street Fighter, with three distinct game modes, including Fighting Ground, World Tour and Battle Hub. Fighting Ground unites core fighting modes from previous titles -- including Arcade Mode, online matches, Training Mode, local versus battles and more -- into one experience which celebrates what fans love about this franchise. World Tour is a single-player immersive story mode that pushes the boundaries of what a fighting game is and allows players to leave their own legacy with their player avatar. Battle Hub provides players with new and unique ways to engage, communicate and interact with the fighting community.

The new Drive System in Street Fighter 6 introduces a gauge used to perform five distinct techniques to enhance a player’s offensive or defensive capabilities, including Drive Impact, Drive Parry, Overdrive Art, Drive Rush and Drive Reversal. The Real Time Commentary Feature incorporates the voices of popular Fighting Game Community (FGC) commentators like Jeremy "Vicious" Lopez and Ryutaro "Aru" Noda directly into the game! Players will have a choice of personalities to narrate matches as they happen with easy-to-understand gameplay explanations. This feature will also support subtitles in 13 languages for increased accessibility. A variety of control schemes allows both new and old fans alike to fight like champs. The Classic Control Type is back alongside a new option. The Modern Control Type allows players to jump into the action with simplified inputs. Special moves are easier to execute with this scheme by combining one button press with a directional input. Players will have a wide range of fighting styles and skills to master, from iconic World Warriors like Ryu and Chun-Li to less seasoned fighters such as Street Fighter V roster Luke or the rookie breakdancer Jamie.
