Descrição

The X-Hunters have returned. Now, they're plotting to resurrect a secret weapon. It is up to Mega Man X to stop them. Luckily, Dr. Light provided new powers and vehicles for Mega Man X; however, they are buried deep below the surface. In addition to Dr. Light's improvements, X can get new weapons by defeating the X-Hunters. All the running, jumping, and shooting action comes to life like never before thanks to advanced graphics technology. Defeat the X-Hunters and save humanity once again in Mega Man X2.
