Descrição

Quarter up for a collection of your favorite Capcom arcade games in your own home with Capcom Arcade Stadium, an arcade gaming app that accesses a library of coin-op classics, all presented in their original arcade playability yet packed with new game features! Start your tour with a free download of 1943: The Battle of Midway, then add on arcade packs to play games from different arcade eras. Add-on packs will give you access to great hits like Bionic Commando, Strider, Super Street Fighter Turbo, and Ghosts ‘n Goblins.

The full arcade experience is available to you, too, with lots of modern features on top of the original games. Game Speed and Difficulty Level can be adjusted, allowing you to slow things down (or speed things up!) to your liking, while a Rewind feature allows you to run it back and try again if things don’t go quite as planned. For the ultimate nostalgia trip, additional Display Settings, Display Filters, and even various frames, including a variety of 3D-rendered arcade cabinets, can add a classic touch to the visuals. Playing games, earning in-game accomplishments, and completing challenges earns CASPO points. As you accrue CASPO, you’ll earn new 2D and 3D frames to display in-game. Those looking to see how they stack up against the competition can claim their place on the online leaderboards in “Timed Challenge” and “Score Challenge” game modes for their favorite titles.
