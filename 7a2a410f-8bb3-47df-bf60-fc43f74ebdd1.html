Descrição

Path of Exile is a free-to-play online action Role-Playing Game (RPG) set in a dark fantasy world. As one of six character classes, players find themselves banished for their past misdeeds to the dark fantasy world of Wraeclast. There, they will encounter hundreds of species of opponents laden with loot and mysterious artifacts as they explore the abandoned continent.
